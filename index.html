about-us

KFC, short for Kentucky Fried Chicken, is a globally recognized fast-food chain specializing in fried chicken. It was founded by Colonel Harland Sanders in 1952, known for its "Original Recipe" with 11 secret herbs and spices. In India, KFC offers a diverse menu, including their signature Hot & Crispy Chicken, Zinger burgers, chicken strips, rice bowls, and various bucket meals. They have numerous outlets across the country and provide options for delivery, pickup, and dine-in.
